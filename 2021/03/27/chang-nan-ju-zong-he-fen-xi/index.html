八、长难句综合分析策略

发表于2021-03-27|更新于2021-03-27|考研英语
|字数总计:587|阅读时长:2分钟|阅读量:

核心战术

​ 假设：句中的that没省略，且用于引导从句。as、for做介词，后跟的是名词。and并列的不是动词或句子，ed结尾的词是done和adj(就先当它不是动词)。

​ 步骤: >step 1.找出句中所有的谓语以及常见从句引导词，并按顺序写下来
​ >step 2.清点谓语、引导词数量并代入等式检测，直至等式成立。
​ 等式：谓语动词数-常见从句引导词数=1
​ → 不满足就是有假设不成立，逐个排除即可

如果结果 ≠ 1
结果＞1 → that省略了，and或but并列谓语了，as、for做引导词了
结果 < 1 → 谓语找漏了(ed结尾的是did)、that做代词、引导词找多了、make sb do sth特殊结构、if done 特殊结构。

​ >step 3.引导词按向后就近原则分配，引导词与紧邻其后出现的国王必属一家。最后剩下的孤家寡人就是主句谓语！
​ >step 4.主句、因果条件类状从、to do优先翻译。

练习：

​ What is harder to establish is whether the productivity revolution that businessmen assume they are presiding over is for real.
​ 找词：what is is whether that assume are is 5-3＝2 → 这里没and，for没有做引导词→那么肯定是that省略了。
​ → 哪里有问题？谓语扎堆的地方有问题！
​ → what is is whether that assume (that) are is 5-4 = 1 √
​ → 就近配对→ (what is) is [whether (that assume) (that are) is ]
​ → What is harder to establish is whether the productivity revolution that businessmen assume (that) they are presiding over is for real.

​ Many European schools introduce the concept of professional training early on in order to make sure children are properly equipped for the professions they want to join.
​ 找词：introduce are want 3-0=0 → 没and，没as和for → that省略了
​ → introduce (that) are (that) want 3-1=1√
​ → 就近配对 → introduce (that are) (that want)
​ → Many European schools introduce the concept of professional training early on in order to make sure (that) children are properly equipped for the professions (that) they want to join.

​ For a while it looked as though the making of semiconductors, which America had invented and which sat at the heart of the new computer age, was going to be the next casualty.
​ 找词：as though which had which sat was 3-3＝0 → looked应该是谓语
​ → looked as though which had which sat was 4-3=1 √
​ → 就近配对 → looked [as though (which had) (which sat) was]
​ → For a while it looked as though the making of semiconductors, which America had invented and which sat at the heart of the new computer age, was going to be the next casualty.
