四、非谓语专题

发表于2021-03-27|更新于2021-03-27|考研英语
|字数总计:1.1k|阅读时长:4分钟|阅读量:

三大非谓语

​ to do（不定式）强调未来性或目的性

​ doing （现在分词）强调正在进行的状态或名词性

​ done （过去分词）强调被动状态或完成、结束的状态

非谓语就是不能做谓语，其余做什么都行

​ ① 做名词（主语、宾语、表语、其他名词）

​ ② 做定语（修饰名词）

​ ③ 其他修饰成分（状语、补语）

(一) 非谓语做名词

非谓语做名词（主语、宾语、表语及其他名词）
仅doing、to do可顶替名词位置

  1. doing 做名词
    doing做名词时，意思最接近其动词本意，故也称为“动名词”

    Seeing is believing.
Learning English requires a lot of effort.

  2. to do做主语、宾语
    To learn English is to understand the world in another language.

    (二) 非谓语做定语（修饰名词）

    前置定语（1个词）；后置定语（多于1个词）

  3. 前置定语
    仅doing、done可做前置定语，用法极为接近一般形容词

    He is a rising star.
You will manage English grammar in the following hours.
She loves the fallen leaves.
We had an organized trip.

    前置定语的用法与意思都像形容词，频繁使用后干脆做个形容词！

    ​ excite v.使兴奋
    ​ The story excites the boy. 这个故事使男孩兴奋

    ​ the exciting story 使人兴奋起来的故事
    ​ → exciting adj.令人兴奋的 The story is exciting(the boy).

    ​ the excited boy 被整兴奋了的男孩
    ​ → excited adj. 兴奋的 The boy is excited(by the story).

    ​ interest v.使感兴趣 interesting 令人感兴趣的 interested 感兴趣的
    ​ 并不是“ing 形容物，ed 形容人”

    ​ Li Ming interests me.
    ​ The interesting Li Ming

  4. 后置定语

    **doing…、done by sth、to do **都可以做后置定语，是难点！
    难点1： done与谓语动词的区分，ed = 形容词？非谓语？动词过去式？

    Much of the language used to describe monetary policy makes it sound like a precise science.
**解决办法：**优先找到一定能是谓语的单词

    难点2： 长难句中的理解和翻译

    The true enemies of science are those who question the evidence

    supporting the pollution of the ozone layer and global warming.

    **解决办法：**修饰性非谓语变从句， <span style='color:red;background:背景颜色;font-size:文字大小;font-family:字体;'>一看到无法理解的修饰性谓语，在前面+that be，先当句子去理解</span>

    后置定语可转换成定语从句理解 +be 补 that

    The man speaking to the teacher is Tom ’s father.
    → The man（that is）speaking to the teacher is Tom ‘s father.

    The girl surrounded by others is Lucy.
    → The girl（that is）surrounded by others is Lucy.

    Much of the language used to describe monetary policy makes it sound like a precise science.
    → Much of the language (that is) used to describe monetary policy makes it sound like a precise science.

    The true enemies of science are those who question the evidence supporting the pollution of the ozone layer and global warming.
    → The true enemies of science are those who question the evidence (that is) supporting the pollution of the ozone layer and global warming.

(三) 非谓语做其他修饰成分（状语、补语）

  1. to do 表目的
    ① to do 表目的（状语）

    <span style='color:blue;background:背景颜色;font-size:文字大小;font-family:字体;'>To improve your English</span>，you need Monkey.
I come to the class <span style='color:blue;background:背景颜色;font-size:文字大小;font-family:字体;'>to study English</span>.

    ② 表意料之外的结果（状语）

    He hurried to the station，<span style='color:blue;background:背景颜色;font-size:文字大小;font-family:字体;'>only to find no bus</span>.
<span style='color:blue;background:背景颜色;font-size:文字大小;font-family:字体;'>Only to find</span> that the joker is me.

  2. doing，done

① **“加be补主” **说得通时（动作发出者通常是主句主语）

​ (He is) Working in the factory，he was an advanced worker.
​ He dropped the glass，(he is) breaking it into pieces.
​ (Microsoft is)Created in 1975，Microsoft has become a tech giant.
​ He is handsome，(he is) envied by many others.

② “加be补主” 说不通时，“加be补我”或“额外补如果”

​ (I am) Judging form his appearance，he must be an actor.
​ (I am) Generally speaking，girls are more careful.
​ (If) Time (is) permitting （us），we ’ll do another two exercise.
​ (If I am)Given more time，I ‘ll be able to do it better.

(四) 三大非谓语的复合语态

​ to do： 我来上课是为了被Monkey指导
​ I come to the class to be guided by Monkey.
​ doing：被Monkey指导着，我感觉很好
​ Being guided by Monkey，I feel good
​ done：我进了一个被Monkey指导的班
​ I have joined a class guided by Monkey.
